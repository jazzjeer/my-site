“From the heart of Punjab to the highlands of Scotland, our story is a rich tapestry of heritage, flavour, and authenticity—where every dish tells a tale of tradition carried across continents.”

At Kohinoor, you’ll find the food of all Punjab. Its kitchens, grills, street stalls and homes. This food evokes fond memories: the simple pleasure of sipping masala chai with buttered buns, the fire of a street-side Vada Pau and the snackery of Bhel, or the fragrant warmth of a potted Biryani shared with friends. Kohinoor serves true Punjabi comfort food inspired by its most treasured corners of the region.

Begin the day with breakfast, which might be a Bacon Naan Roll, a laden-plate of Akuri or a bowl of Date & Banana Porridge. Then, lunch lightly on a Paneer Roomali Roll or fancy Salads. Unfurl luxuriously into the afternoon with sips of Chai. The evening brings smoky, melt-in-mouth grills, robust and spicy curries. Come, savour Makhmali Paneer and Goan Monkfish Curry.
